Boise, Idaho

Location

Boise, also known as the "City of Trees", is the capital and most populated city in the state of Idaho.

About

Outdoor Recreation

Boise is known for many outdoor activities like hiking, skiing, mountain biking, and fishing

Sports

While Boise doesn't have any professional sports teams, we do enjoy watching Boise State football, the minor league hockey Idaho Steelheads, and the minor league baseball Boise Hawks.

Arts

Boise is home to many different music and art festivals such as Treefort Music Festival, Boise Music Festival, Art in the Park, and Hyde Park Street Fair

Things to do

  • Floating the Boise River
  • Hike Tablerock
  • Find a new favorite restaurant in Downtown Boise
